Simple Apollo Noodles

This holds a special place in most Mauritians heart and also mine. Because it is cheap and very easy to make; because you only need very few things!
Hence, I will be following the "tutorial" because we are CHADS here!

Things You Need

  • Apollo Noodle Packet
  • Water
  • Seasoning Packet inside the Apollo Noodle Packet
  • Heat

Steps

  1. Put the water to a boil
  2. Now open the packet and place the dried noodle in the boiling water
  3. Let it cook for about 3 mins
  4. After cooking / boiling open the seasoning packet and add it to the pot
  5. Then you mix it with a fork or chop sticks and eat it
